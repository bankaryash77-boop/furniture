Who We Are

Furniture Born From
Passion & Purpose

We started Furni. with a simple belief: that great design shouldn't be a luxury. Every chair, every sofa, every table we create is the result of hundreds of hours of design thinking, material research, and craftsmanship.

We work with artisans across India and Europe to ensure that every piece that arrives at your home is built to last a lifetime — and to look beautiful while doing it.
